About the Artist

I grew up in Pensacola, Florida, and music has been at the center of my life for as long as I can remember. I studied music composition at the University of Northern Colorado, and in 2018 you might have caught me on NBC's The Voice.

I started playing piano when I was 11. I tuned my first piano more than a decade ago. I'll be honest: I made it worse. But that failure was the spark. Every piano has its own personality: a unique feel, sound, look, resonance, hum, and history.

When you hire me, you're not just getting a technician — you're inviting someone who listens closely, responds sensitively, and helps your piano find its natural voice again.
